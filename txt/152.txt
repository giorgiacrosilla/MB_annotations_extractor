Wednesday, July 10, 1895, Hotel Marienbad, München
We reached here at 5 and Obrist met us, dressed in a jade-green suit, looking pretty and jolly. We dined with him at the Maximilien Café and talked a good deal. He seemed at times on the point of using Bernhard’s formulas, but never attained it.
Thursday, July 11, 1895, Munich
I called on Miss Sellers and had some most amusing gossip. Particularly funny was her account of a Furtwangler reconciliation scene.
