Thursday, May 3, 1894, Florence [crossed out]
Friday, June 1, 1894, Florence
Nearly a month’s interval. We have been living very quietly, I seeing practically nobody but Obrist (Bernhard of course), but him every day, while Bernhard has been going out more than his wont under the wing of Carlo Placci and meeting charming people like the Countess Rasponi, the Baroness French, Signor Nencioni, Prince Galitzine, etc. He has enjoyed it.
