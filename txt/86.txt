Monday, January 14, 1895, Lion d’Or, Reims
Still here, and still happy. We have enjoyed the Cathedral au fond, and each other very much.
Tuesday, January 15, 1895, Victoria, Bâle
Bernhard and I left at 7.0 in the morning, and HO was going to Paris in the 8.12 train.
We came here, reading La fin du Paganisme (Boissier) and James’ Psychology.  Arriving here, we found the line broken by an avalanche, and so have to stay over.
