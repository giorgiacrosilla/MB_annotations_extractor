After the earthquake he brought them each a little wax taper telling them to burn it reciting an Ave, after having taken a purge, in order to keep off another shock of earthquake. He is fearfully in debt, but he pretends that his creditors who come and stand patiently about, like saints in niches, waiting for small payments on account, are very grand people, “Professori di Matematica” etc. a naive fraud which takes in no one.
In the evening Placci came to dinner, and he was quite delightful, so sympathetic about Bernhard’s ideas on art, and so entertaining in gossip.
