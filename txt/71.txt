 Yes, it is clear if I wish to be a thinking being I must metaphysicize.
Obrist left Sept. 7. Christina Bremner arrived on the 8th and we had a week in Dresden, a day in the Spreewald, 4 days in Berlin, and 8 in Holland. I enjoyed Franz Hals to the full. Then home until Nov. (for this time see file of B.B.’s letters, and the children’s “Thinking Lessons”)
Nov. 4 I came to Paris and stayed with Berty Russell until the 14th, a dear, dear boy, and very clever.  Then I went to Munich and saw H[ermann] O[brist] and made some friends there, especially Miss Goudstikker.
