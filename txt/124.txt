Tuesday, May 7, 1895, Villa Rosa, Fiesole
Poets are people who go straight from feeling to expression, not via thought.
One’s results are, after all, only moments in one’s thoughts. 
Friday, May 10, 1895 
I have not written - I have just been struggling to live - that is to keep happy and feel a springing up of vitality - so necessary to “living” in any satisfactory sense - I had a rather knock-down blow, although it ought not to have been unexpected, in realizing that the children must be inevitably trained as Catholics.
