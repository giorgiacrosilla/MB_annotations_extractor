Monday, December 10, 1894, 3 rue de Beaune, Paris 
Went with Reinach to the Luxembourg, and went all over with him and M. Bénédite. 
Got awfully tired, for they made us look at all the bad pictures and statuary. 
Tuesday, December 11, 1894
Worked on Louvre drawings - went to Durand-Ruel’s after lunch - Mrs. Hapgood called. 
Wednesday, December 12, 1894
Called on Reinach - went with him. Sturges came for the evening.
Thursday, December 13, 1894 
Alys’ wedding day! Wired “Cheer up” and received this reply: 
[A telegram pasted in: ‘Feeling better = Bore and Loo Russell +’]
