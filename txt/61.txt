Friday, June 22, 1894, Strassburg
A nice day of loafing and talking. We looked at the pictures, and enjoyed the Carianis and Theotocopuli and I discovered a Francesco Cotignola. Wandered about the “Kleine Frankreich”, but decided that we no longer care for the dirty and squalid picturesque.
Saturday, June 23, 1894, Strassburg
Saw the cathedral again, from the clock transept, and were very much impressed by it. The lighting was very fine, with the door open, bringing out the great column in full relief
