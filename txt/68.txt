Wednesday, August 29, 1894, Corneille, Paris
Had a fine galop round the Louvre in the morning, and an hour or so in the Cast Collection in the Trocadero in the afternoon. I was unwell and came home, while Bernhard haunted the bookstalls of the Odéon. He bought the new Maeterlinck, and we read “ Alladine e Pallamides ” in the Luxembourg, and fought over it, Bernhard defending it. He grows too tolerant!!
